Our project, the Smart Farming System, integrates IoT and modern technology to revolutionize traditional farming practices. Key features include:

  • ESP32-based control system

  • Automated irrigation system controlled via an Android app
  • Firebase integration for seamless data storage and retrieval

IoT-Based Fire Safety System

I secured 1st position in the Electronics Gadget Making event at Tech-O-Tsav, my college's annual tech fest. For this event, I developed an IoT-based Fire Safety Device using:

  • ESP32 for control and connectivity,
  • DHT11 for temperature sensing, and
  • Blynk IoT Cloud for real-time monitoring.
The device autonomously sprinkles water during fire hazards while simultaneously updating temperature data on the Blynk platform, showcasing a practical solution for fire safety in smart systems.

Smart Security System (INSPIRE Award)

My project is an innovative Smart Security System that earned me selection as a State-Level Finalist in the INSPIRE Awards 2022, organized by the Department of Science and Technology, Government of India. The system integrates RFID with IoT (ESP8266 with Google Sheets) and a GSM module, designed for home lockers, workplace safes, and strong rooms. It logs credentials of authorized personnel with time and date stamps, storing the data in real-time on Google Sheets. In the event of unauthorized or suspicious activity, the system triggers an alarm and immediately alerts the authorized user via a phone call, ensuring enhanced security through IoT-driven monitoring.
